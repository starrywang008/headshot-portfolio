About关于

Hi, I'm Starry. 你好，我是 Starry。

Hi, I'm Starry — a photographer based in the SF Bay Area. I grew up in a coastal city in Guangdong, southern China, and picked up a camera for the first time three years ago in New York. What started as a way to see the city slowly became something I couldn't put down. 你好，我是 Starry——旧金山湾区的形象照摄影师。我来自广东南方的一座海滨城市，三年前在纽约第一次拿起相机，从此一发不可收拾。

My background in education and learning design shapes how I work: I know how to read a room, make people feel at ease, and find the moment when your real personality shows up on camera. A great headshot has opened doors for my clients — new jobs, speaking invitations, brand collaborations. I want that for you too. 我的教育与学习体验设计背景影响了我拍照的方式：我擅长观察人、营造放松的氛围，总能捕捉到你最真实的那一面。我的客户靠着一张好的形象照收到了新工作邀约、演讲机会和品牌合作机会。我也希望这发生在你身上。
